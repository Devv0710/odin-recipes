Lasagna

Desciption

The following content describe how to do a lasagna from scratch.

This recipe don't take more than 1 hour, and i so delicious

Ingredients

  • Ground meat(500gr)
  • Tomatoes 2
  • Onion 2
  • Condiments
  • Lasagna pasta 1
  • Ham and cheese (300gr each )
  • Catsup 1
  • Milk cream 1

Steps

  1. Cut the onion and tomato into small pieces
  2. Cook the meat and add the seasonings you prefer
  3. Put the onion to cook with the meat
  4. When the onion is on the point put the tomato and the catsup
  5. When you finsih the process of cook, select a baking dish
  6. Put first the milk cream
  7. Put the lasagna pasta until you fill a layer
  8. Put the meat over the layer
  9. Put ham and cheese
  10. finally put again the milk cream, and repeat the process untill you finish you meat
