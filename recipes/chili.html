Vegetarian Chili

Description

This hearty vegetarian chili is packed with flavor and perfect for a comforting meal. It's easy to make and can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil 2 tablespoons
  • Onion 1, chopped
  • Bell pepper (red, green, or yellow) 1, chopped
  • Garlic 2 cloves, minced
  • Kidney beans (canned, drained) 1 can (15 oz)
  • Black beans (canned, drained) 1 can (15 oz)
  • Corn (canned, drained) 1 can (15 oz) / diced tomatoes (canned) 1 can (14.5 oz) undrained
  • Vegetable broth 2 cups
  • Chili powder 2 tablespoons
  • Cumin 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • (Optional) Toppings: shredded cheese, sour cream, avocado, chopped fresh cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and bell pepper. Cook for 5-7 minutes, or until softened.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
  4. Stir in kidney beans, black beans, corn, diced tomatoes, vegetable broth, chili powder, and cumin.
  5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20-25 minutes, or until thickened.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with your favorite toppings.
