Cheesy Tomato Pasta Bake

Description

This comforting Cheesy Tomato Pasta Bake is a quick and easy weeknight meal perfect for the whole family. It's loaded with pasta, a flavorful tomato sauce, and plenty of melty cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (elbow, penne, or rotini) 8 oz (uncooked)
  • Olive oil 2 tablespoons
  • Onion 1, chopped
  • Garlic 2 cloves, minced
  • (Optional) Ground meat (beef, sausage, or turkey) 1 pound, browned and crumbled
  • Crushed tomatoes (canned) 1 (28-ounce) can
  • Tomato paste 1 tablespoon
  • Italian seasoning 1 teaspoon
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Milk 1 cup
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese 2 cups
  • Parmesan cheese 1/2 cup

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  4. Add onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Add garlic and cook for 30 seconds, or until fragrant.
  6. (Optional) If using meat, add it to the skillet and cook until browned and crumbled.
  7. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper.
  8. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta and tomato sauce.
  10. Stir in 1 cup of milk and 1 cup of mozzarella cheese.
  11. Pour the mixture into a greased baking dish.
  12. Top with remaining mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese.
  13. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  14. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving.
